A Thousand Year Old Vampire

A Solo RPG Playthrough


Name: Zhou Mingzhe (周明哲)

Birth Year: 847 CE

Origin: Chang’an, China


Background:

Born the son of a minor tax collector, Zhou became a tutor of a merchant family after failing the Imperial exams multiple times, bringing shame to his family’s name. He nearly died of starvation in a devastating famine during the reign of Emperor Xizong in 874 CE. A humble coffin-maker found him and, accidentally thinking Zhou was dead, attempted to prepare him for burial. However, he was interrupted by a group of pillagers who sacked his workshop and murdered him. Due to the burial rites not being properly completed, Zhou rose from death as a Jiangshi, the hopping undead. His lower body rigid and stiff, he is cursed to hobble throughout the land at the mercy of strangers, forever tormented by a terrible hunger for their qi. This is his story...

Characters

  • Master Chen: my Confucian tutor, still alive in Chang'an
  • Zhou Wei: my younger brother, now head of the family
  • Li Cheng: the merchant's son and my appointed pupil, rather dull-witted
  • Chen Mu: the coffin-maker who attempted to bury me, also turned Jiangshi after his violent demise

Skills

  • Master Calligrapher: naturally talented and able to create beautiful scripts with brush and ink
  • Learned Litterateur: knows all classic Chinese literature both inside and out
  • Confucian Scholar: Very well versed in the teachings of Confucius, much to my chagrin as I know exactly what burial rites were botched on me.
  • Careful Coroner: Able to medically determine if someone is terminally ill or near death so I can target them for feeding
